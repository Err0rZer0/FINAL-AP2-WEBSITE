Masungi Georeserve

A conservation adventure park with hanging bridges, trails, and limestone formations.

About Masungi Georeserve

Masungi Georeserve blends environmental protection with thrilling exploration. Walk through a living museum of karst outcrops, suspension bridges, and forest viewpoints.

Location

Sitio Kalupang, Brgy. San Luis, Baras, Rizal

Best Time to Visit

December to April

Entrance Fee

₱150 per person (guide required)

Opening Hours

8:00 AM – 5:00 PM

Facebook Page

https://www.facebook.com/MasungiGeoreserve

Activities
  • Hiking
  • Canopy walk
  • Guided eco tours
Travel Tips
  • Book a tour in advance
  • Wear closed shoes
  • Bring light rain gear
